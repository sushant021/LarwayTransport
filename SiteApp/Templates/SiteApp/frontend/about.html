Who We Are

Larway Transportation began operations in 1985 in a shared office suite in Oakridges, Ontario with little more than a hard-line telephone, a Rolodex, and their word to focus on people and their promise and not just cargo. Within the first year of operation, Larway crossed their million-dollar promise and continued to grow. Since then, the company has adapted to meet the needs of their customers and their drivers, leading the way in safety and reliability. In a constantly changing and developing industry, one of Larway’s most important qualities continues to be their word – and that will never change.

Co Founder at Larway

Larry Labatt

Founding Member

After driving trucks for 18 years and working as a dispatcher, Larry knew the logistics and transportation business inside and out. He was tired of the daily grind and wanted a change and a better life for himself and his family. Using his knowledge of the industry and his drive, Larry began to lay the foundation for his own company.

Catherine Labatt

Founding Member

Catherine, Larry’s wife, went to school for business and had experience running a fitness studio franchise. She took the idea of a new company and made it a reality. Scrawling a detailed business plan on a large piece of paper, they took it to the bank to ask for a start-up loan of $5,000. She also made a promise to make 1 million dollars in the first year. Their confidence and organization got them the loan, and the foundation was set.

Stuart Pound

Founding Member

Stuart Pound was the piece that completed the Larway puzzle. Starting with a hardline phone and a Rolodex filled with meaningful contacts Stuart, fulfilling the role of sales manager for the new company, began making the necessary connections. A year after the founding members entered the bank to ask for their loan, they had fulfilled their promise of making a million dollars!
